Salar Jung Museum

It has a collection of sculptures, paintings, carvings, textiles, manuscripts, ceramics, metallic artifacts, carpets, clocks, and furniture from Japan, China, Burma, Nepal, India, Persia, Egypt, Europe, and North America. It is one of the largest museums in the world. The museum has a famous Quran collection from around the world and different fonts and designs, called the Quran Written with Gold and Silver.
